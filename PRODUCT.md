# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Vietnamese-speaking adults aged 18+ who are comparing slot/jackpot entertainment options, reading beginner guides, or looking for a safe route to the partner gaming platform. They usually arrive from search or mobile browsing, want fast orientation, and need clear reminders about risk, budget limits, and external-platform disclosure.

## Product Purpose

Thantai88Slots is a standalone Vietnamese slots/iGaming content and affiliate site in the Thantai88 ecosystem. It should explain slot concepts, surface relevant lobby/provider categories from the partner platform, support evergreen SEO authority, and direct qualified visitors to the gaming site through honest CTAs. Success means the homepage immediately communicates what the site covers, highlights useful content after the hero, and feels credible enough for a client design review without claiming guaranteed wins, exact RTP, licenses, payouts, or promotions.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, polished, and careful. The brand can feel casino-bright and premium, but its editorial voice stays calm, Vietnamese-first, and risk-aware.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dark casino templates with contextless “777” hype, endless identical cards, fake urgency, fake jackpots, unsupported “best slot” claims, and English UI leftovers. Avoid design patterns that make the site look like a dashboard or crypto app; this is a landing/content surface. Avoid copy that sounds internally generated, such as “SEO content,” “API extracted,” “placeholder,” or “CTA.”

## Design Principles

1. Lead with context: explain that this is the slots-focused Thantai88 guide and that gameplay happens on the partner platform.
2. Put fresh/useful content right after the hero so the site feels maintained, not empty.
3. Use partner-platform evidence conservatively: provider/lobby names may guide categorization, but never become payout, RTP, licensing, or ranking claims.
4. Make every conversion moment responsible: 18+, budget limits, and affiliate/external-platform disclosure stay visible.
5. Build authority through useful Vietnamese education before asking for a click.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, readable Vietnamese typography, keyboard-accessible navigation/CTA controls, and reduced-motion support. Avoid color-only meaning, avoid tiny low-contrast text, and keep mobile tap targets comfortable.
