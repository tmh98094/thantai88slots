## Safe use in content

- Use these names as “lobby/provider categories visible in the partner platform bundle.”
- Do not claim exact availability, RTP, volatility, jackpot size, payout speed, licensing, legality, or “best provider” unless the client supplies an operator brief or a current public source.
- Do not build “hot today” pages from this bundle alone.
- Best homepage use: a provider/lobby context strip that explains what readers should check before playing.
- Best article use: evergreen guides such as “cách chọn sảnh slot,” “RTP là gì,” “đọc điều kiện jackpot,” and “kiểm tra ưu đãi trước khi nhận.”
